introduction

Tiger’s Eye is one of the teams under FWD Life Insurance Corporation-Paragon Life and Wealth Coaches. Tiger’s Eye is led by Coach Jerome Astorga who is a law graduate, Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) member, and a training supervisor. Tiger’s Eye is composed of multi-talented and passionate individuals who came from different fields. Fueled by their passion and willingness to improve their quality of life while also helping other people improve theirs, they have decided to come together to fulfill the team’s mission and vision.
